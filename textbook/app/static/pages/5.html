Proportion Playground Task

Paint Splash

  1. Discuss with your group: How are the color: color ratios different for each paint splash? How are they the same?
  2. Discuss with your group: How would you describe the paint splash you created? What kind of rule can describe how to make your paint color? Share your responses.
  3. As a group: create a rule using words and an equation using the y=mx format
    4. Table/Graph.
      Discuss with your group:
    • What does the x represent in your graph?
    • What does the y represent in your graph?
    • How is the y = mx rule related to the blue:yellow ratios from your paint splashes? Does this make sense, why or why not?
    • How could you use your y = mx rule to find another paint splash that matches the color? (Then check a sample in your graph to see if it fits!)
