Paint Splash

  2. Discuss with your group: How would you describe the paint splash you created? What kind of rule can describe how to make your paint color? digital anchored commenting The Recorder should type your response(s) in complete sentences in the Activity Feed.
  3. As a group: create a rule using words and an equation using the y=mx format
      4. Discuss with your group:
    • What does the x represent in your graph?
    • What does the y represent in your graph?
    • How is the y = mx rule related to the blue:yellow ratios from your paint splashes? Why does this make sense?
