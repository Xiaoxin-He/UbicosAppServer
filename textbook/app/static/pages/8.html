Paint Splash

Create Your Own Color

I love my Perfect Purple Paint, but you probably want to come up with your own awesome color for your house. To do that, we’re going to use Paint Splash. Working as a group in Paint Splash in the single paint splash mode, create a paint splash of your choice. Your group needs to agree on the color.

Each group member should then use the double paint splash to recreate your group’s paint splash on the left. And then on the right create either a larger or smaller matching batch (each group member should create a different sized matching paint splash on the right).

Take a screenshot of one person’s screen to post and upload to your Gallery #2.
