Proportion Playground Task Card

Paint Splash

  1. Assign roles to each group member:
    • Facilitator
    • Creation Surveyor
    • Graphing Expert
    • Recorder
  2. Create a paint splash of your choice. Once your group agrees on the design, the Creation Surveyor should take a screenshot and paste it into your group’s gallery.
  3. Discuss with your group: How would you describe the paint splash you created? digital anchored commenting The Recorder should type your response(s) in complete sentences in the gallery.
  4. Each group member should then...
    • Create a new, unique paint splash (either larger or smaller batch from all others in your group) with the same color (except the Creation Surveyor who will use the original paint splash). Screenshot your creation & paste into your gallery. Gallery
    • Record the ratio of blue to yellow in the table.
    • Record your group’s blue and yellow values and have the graphing expert post a screenshot of your group’s table and graph to your gallery.
    • Create a Whiteboard?
  5. Discuss with your group: How are the blue:yellow ratios different for each paint splash? How are they the same?
  6. Discuss with your group: How are the red:blue ratios different for each necklace? How are they the same?
