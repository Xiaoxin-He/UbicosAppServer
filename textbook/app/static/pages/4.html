Proportion Playground Task

Paint Splash

  1. Assign roles to each group member:
    • Facilitator
    • Creation Surveyor
    • Graphing Expert
    • Recorder
  2. In the single paint splash mode, create a paint splash of your choice. Your group needs to agree on the color.
  3. Each group member should then use the double paint splash setting to...
    • Create a new, unique paint splash (either larger or smaller batch from all others in your group) with the same color and amount on the left side, but with different amounts of paint on the right side. Choose one person’s screen to post and paste into your activity feed. Gallery
    • Record the ratio of each color in the table.
    • Record your group’s color values and have the graphing expert post a screenshot of your group’s table and graph to your activity feed.
  4. Discuss with your group: How are the color:color ratios different for each paint splash? How are they the same?
  5. Discuss with your group: How would you describe the paint splash you created? What kind of rule can describe how to make your paint color? digital anchored commenting The Recorder should type your response(s) in complete sentences in the Activity Feed.
