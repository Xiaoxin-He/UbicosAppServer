Proportion Playground Task

Scene: Necklace Creator

  1. Assign roles to each group member in your Google Doc:
    • Facilitator
    • Creation Surveyor
    • Desmos Expert
    • Recorder
  2. Create a necklace with some kind of repeating pattern. Once your group agrees on the design, the Creation Surveyor should take a screenshot and paste it into your Doc.
  3. Discuss with your group: How would you describe the pattern you created? The Recorder should type your response(s) in complete sentences.
  4. Each group member should then...
    • Create a new, unique necklace (either larger or smaller from all others in your group) with the same repeating pattern (except the Creation Surveyor who will use the original necklace). Screenshot your creation & paste into the gallery. gallery
    • Record the ratio of red beads to blue beads in the appropriate place.
    • Tell the Desmos Expert your red and blue values to record in a Desmos table.
  5. Discuss with your group: How are the red:blue ratios different for each necklace? How are they the same?
