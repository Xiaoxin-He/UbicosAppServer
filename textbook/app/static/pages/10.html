Create your own color

I love my Perfect Purple Paint, but you probably want to come up with your own awesome color for your house. Check out Paint Splash here!

As a group, create a color splash of your choice in the Paint Splash tool. Each group member should use the double paint splash setting to recreate your group’s paint splash on the left. Then on the right create either a larger or smaller matching batch (each group member should create a different sized matching paint splash on the right). After you are done, enter all the group members ratios into a Paint Splash Table.

Next, Create a whiteboard. Remember, your whiteboard needs:

  • A Title
  • A screenshot of one person’s double paint splash (2 different sized batches) (check here how to take screenshot)
  • A Screenshot of the table that lists your group members’ batches
  • A screenshot of your graph
  • A written explanation about how you know how to find any size batch for your color.
  • A rule in words and numbers.

Take a screenshot of your whiteboard and upload the screenshot in the Paint Splash Gallery. Every member of your group will upload an image of your groups’ board.
