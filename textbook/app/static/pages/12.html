Activity 1 - How Fast?

Lab

In Person

  1. Using the materials at hand, your task is to devise a method of determining how fast your car moves as confidently as possible. Create your plan on the large whiteboard.
    • What measurements do you need to take?
    • What units will you use?
    • Do you plan to take more than one set of data?
  2. Take a picture of your directions so you can reference them as you do the experiment.

Digital

  1. Carry out your plan from question 1 and record your data here Table/Graph.
  2. As a group, create a Whiteboard to share with the rest of the class. It should include:
    • Data table
    • Graph with units and labels
    • The story of your car. What did you observe? How did it move? How do you know?
