Activity 1 - How Fast?

Lab

In Person

  1. Using the materials at hand, our task is to devise a method of determining how fast our car moves as confidently as possible. let's create our class plan on a large whiteboard.
    • What measurements should we take?
    • What units will we use?
    • Do we plan to take more than one set of data?
    • What procedures do we need to follow so that our data is as accurate and comparable as possible?
  2. Create a table on your whiteboard to collect your data.
  3. Let’s take a picture of our directions so you can reference them as you do the experiment. [add gallery]

Digital

  1. Carry out your plan from step 1 and record your data on a whiteboard. When you have collected your data, put it into the table in your Model Book. Table/Graph.
  2. As a group, create a Whiteboard to share with the rest of the class. It should include:
    • Data table
    • Graph with units and labels
    • The story of your car. What did you observe? How did it move? How do you know?
  3. Take a screenshot of the whiteboard with your group’s data, and post it to our gallery. Group A or Group B or Group C.
