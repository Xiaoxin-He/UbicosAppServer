Day 4

Activity 1 - How Fast?

Pre-lab Discussion

  1. Observe a moving object, (ex, toy car, remote operated toy, or even a video clip of an object moving). Video Ask yourself, “How can we find its speed?”

  2. Share your ideas and brainstorm as a class how you know the object is moving.

  3. Ask yourself, how could we figure out this toy car’s speed?

Materials

  • Toy battery powered car
  • Stopwatch
  • Meter stick
  • Masking tape
  • Whiteboard
  • Whiteboard Markers
