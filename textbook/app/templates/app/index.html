2.1 Constant Velocity

Activity 1- How Fast?

Pre-lab discussion

  1. Observe a moving object, (ex, toy car, remote operated toy, or even a video clip of an object moving). Ask yourself, “How do you know this object is moving?”
  2. Share some of your ideas with a partner. Then share some of your ideas with the whole class the the Chat function and brainstorm.
  3. Now watch the battery operated vehicle run on the floor or table. How could we figure out this toy car’s speed?
  4. Conduct a class brainstorm and record their ideas at the front of the room.
  5. Conduct a class brainstorm and upload your picture in the gallery.
  6. Take a picture
  7. Visit the Gallery.

Apparatus:

  • Toy battery powered car that travels at constant speed (alternatively have someone walk at a roughly constant pace)
  • Stopwatch
  • Meter stick
  • Masking tape or whiteboard markers
  • Copies of 2.1 Act 1 How Fast worksheet
  • Copies of 2.2 Act 1 Number Line Speed
  • Copies of WS2 Terms for How Far
